Manipulating Lists (#4)

Implement the function getFriendSuggestions(). It should return friends of friends of the person, excluding those people who are his or her friends already. For instance, for the following friend connections:

Alice - Bob
Bob - Charlie

getFriendSuggestions() should return Charlie for Alice, because Charlie is a friend of Alice's friend Bob and isn't yet a friend of Alice.

No friend suggestions for Alice in the following case, Bob and Charlie are already her friends:

Alice - Bob
Alice - Charlie
Bob - Charlie
