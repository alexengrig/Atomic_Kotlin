Mastering the IDE: Analyze Stacktrace

You can automatically navigate to the line where an exception was thrown by clicking on it in the stacktrace. Run Task.kt to see the stacktrace and navigate to different lines.

You can also analyze the stacktrace copied from another place, like a bug tracker. Copy the following stacktrace and call the action Analyze stacktrace:

Exception in thread "main" java.lang.IllegalStateException: something is wrong
	at stacktrace.TaskKt.foo(Task.kt:4)
	at stacktrace.TaskKt.bar(Task.kt:8)
	at stacktrace.TaskKt.main(Task.kt:12)
